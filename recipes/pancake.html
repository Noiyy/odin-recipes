Pancake

Description

Very simple & yummy pancake recipe anyone can make! :)

Ingredients

  • 500ml milk
  • 10 tbsp flour
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 packaging vanilla sugar
  • a bit of salt

Steps

  1. We'll prepare the Ingredients
  2. Put all ingredients in one bowl
  3. Mix the dough with mixer and we can let it sit for atleast 15-20min
  4. After 20min, check if dough has the right consistency and we can bake it. We grease the pan with oil, not too much, and heat the pan thoroughly, then we can bake.
  5. After they're done, we can season them according to our taste.
